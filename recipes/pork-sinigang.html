Pork Sinigang

Pork sinigang is a Filipino sour soup, typically built around tamarind, with pork belly or ribs simmered with vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly or pork ribs, cut into chunks
  • 1 packet (40-50g) tamarind soup base (sinigang mix), or fresh tamarind
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2-3 tomatoes, quartered
  • 6-8 cups water
  • 1 radish (labanos), sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • A handful of string beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch lengths
  • A bunch of kangkong (water spinach) or spinach
  • 2-3 pieces green finger chilies (siling pansigang)
  • Fish sauce (patis), to taste
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
